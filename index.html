WELCOME TO MYTHERIA

Mytheria is a Blockchain Strategy Game revolves around a core loop of engaging gaming activities such as collecting, upgrading, battling, and trading/socializing with other players for Gods, Mortals and Mytherias - the Ulimate Power of Gods.

With the concept similar to a MOBA game (Multiplayer online battle arena), Mytheria brings to its users thrilling feelings of building an army of Gods to win battles against other players. With hundreds of Gods, Mortals to collect, each God have multiple ways to build and fit to different Roles and many Game Modes, players are truly free to choose how they want to enjoy Mytheria.

Play & Earn
With various game modes
  • PvE
    Level-up your Gods and face infinite challenges in the Olympus. The Higher you climb, the higher Reward you get.
  • PvP
    Competitive Mode where you hone your skills and strategy against other players. Stay at the Top to earn MYTH at the end of each Season.
  • Battle Royale
    Chaotic fun in Battle Royale against other 7 Players. Be creative to be the only Champion and earn Rewards based on your performance.
  • E-SPORT
    Best players in each Region will participate in Mtheria's global tournaments, with a crowdfunded prize pool.
Choose Your Pantheon
The world of Mytheria feature hundreds of Gods and Mortals from many Pantheons, each one have advantages and specialities. Choose your favorite deities and build the best alliance for different Game Modes to bring out their full potential.
